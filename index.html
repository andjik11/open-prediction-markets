Open Prediction Markets

An open-source prediction market for collective intelligence, transparent forecasting, and public-good research.

What is this project?

Open Prediction Markets is a Web3 public-good initiative focused on building an open, transparent, and decentralized prediction market infrastructure.

Prediction markets aggregate collective knowledge to forecast future events. When designed responsibly, they become powerful tools for research, education, and decision-making.

Why it matters

  • Open-source alternatives to centralized platforms
  • Transparent and verifiable market mechanisms
  • Collective intelligence over speculation
  • Public-interest forecasting and research

Current status

  • Concept and research phase
  • Market mechanism exploration
  • Oracle and dispute resolution analysis

Funding usage

  • Protocol and smart contract development
  • Security research and audits
  • Documentation and open-source tooling
  • Community governance experiments

Open & transparent

All core components of this project are planned to be open-source and publicly auditable. Progress updates will be shared openly with the community.
