What is this project?

Open Prediction Markets is a public-good Web3 initiative building decentralized and transparent prediction market infrastructure.

Roadmap

  • Phase 1: Research & market design
  • Phase 2: MVP smart contracts (testnet)
  • Phase 3: Oracle & dispute mechanisms
  • Phase 4: Public beta & governance experiments

Open & transparent

All core components will be open-source. Development progress and financial transparency will be shared publicly.
